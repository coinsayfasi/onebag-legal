Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Summary: The One Bag mobile app does not rely on browser cookies. This page explains how cookies may be used on our website and how you can control them.

This Cookie Policy applies to the One Bag website and any future web experiences operated by One Bag Travel. It does not change the way your data is handled inside the mobile app. For app-level data practices, see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Summary: Cookies are small text files stored by your browser to remember settings, sessions, or preferences.

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They can help websites remember user preferences, keep sessions active, measure performance, and improve usability.

2. Cookies in the One Bag Mobile App

Summary: The mobile app does not use browser cookies because it is not a browser-based product.

The One Bag iOS and Android apps do not use browser cookies. Some third-party SDKs used in the app, such as analytics, crash reporting, subscriptions, or advertising services, may use device identifiers or local storage mechanisms appropriate for mobile platforms. Those practices are described in the Privacy Policy, not in this cookie policy.

3. Cookies on the Website

Summary: Our public legal website is minimal. If cookies are used, they are limited to essential website functionality unless consent is required and obtained.
CategoryPurposeRequired

At the time of this update, our legal website is intentionally lightweight. If non-essential cookies are introduced later, we will present appropriate notice and consent controls where required by law.

4. Third-Party Services

Summary: Third-party providers may set their own cookies when embedded or linked services are used.

If we embed or connect to third-party services on the website in the future, those services may set cookies subject to their own policies. Examples may include analytics tools, customer support widgets, or video/content embeds.

5. How to Control Cookies

Summary: You can manage or delete cookies from your browser settings at any time.
  • Block all cookies through your browser settings
  • Delete previously stored cookies
  • Configure alerts before a website stores a cookie
  • Limit third-party cookies only

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Your Rights

Summary: Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to receive notice, give or withhold consent, and request deletion of related personal data.

Depending on your location, privacy laws such as GDPR, CCPA, or KVKK may give you rights related to cookies and tracking technologies, including the right to withdraw consent where applicable.
