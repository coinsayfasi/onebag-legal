Data Deletion Request

Last updated: February 26, 2026

One Bag respects your right to control your personal data. This page explains how to delete your data from our services.

Option 1: Delete from Within the App

  1. Open One Bag
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

This will permanently delete your account and all associated cloud data (trips, packing items, traveler data, forget scores, categories). The deletion is processed within 30 days.

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

Send an email to: support.onebag@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your user ID (found in Settings > Account)
  • Confirmation that you want all data deleted

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email.

Local Data

Data stored only on your device (Isar database, document vault files) is not accessible to us. To remove local data:

  • Clear app data: Device Settings > Apps > One Bag > Clear Data
  • Uninstall the app: This removes all local data including vault documents

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel active subscriptions. Please cancel your subscription separately:

  • iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > One Bag > Cancel
  • Android: Google Play Store > Subscriptions > One Bag > Cancel
